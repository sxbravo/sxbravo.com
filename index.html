What Is Bravo Glassware?

Crafted Glassware, Global Quality

Bravo Glassware is a professional manufacturer focusing on wine glasses, water cups, plates, vases and various daily glass products. We adhere to exquisite craftsmanship, diverse styles and strict quality control, supplying premium glassware for global wholesale and custom clients.

  • 2018
  • Bravo Glassware was founded in 2018. Founded in 2018, Bravo Glassware was born from a passion for exquisite glass craftsmanship and a vision to bring high-quality, elegant glassware to global markets.
    Driven by the pursuit of premium design, fine workmanship and reliable product quality, the founder committed to creating sophisticated glassware that blends artistry with practicality.
    With an aim to connect outstanding Chinese glass manufacturing with worldwide high-end buyers, we officially established Bravo Glassware in 2018, focusing on custom glassware, home decoration glass and luxury tableware for global clients.
  • 2020
  • The company maintains long-term cooperation with over 300 mid-to-high-end clients across the globe. Annually, we establish stable partnerships with over 300 mid-to-high-end clients across the globe.
    Our glassware products are exported to numerous countries and regions worldwide, enjoying steady sales volume and high market recognition.
    We specialize in original design, customized production and premium quality control, consistently delivering sophisticated glassware that meets diverse market and customer demands.Relying on exquisite craftsmanship, competitive pricing and professional one-stop service, we have gained long-term trust, repeat orders and widespread praise from global buyers.
  • 2025
  • One-stop customized services and strict quality control to achieve win-win cooperation with global clients. We serve a wide spectrum of industries covering retail, catering & entertainment, brand customization, event & tournament supplies, and home lifestyle sectors.
    Our clients include food and beverage manufacturers, cosmetics factories, professional wholesalers, supermarket chains, gift shops and liquor stores.
    We also provide dedicated glassware for bars, restaurants and wedding industries.
    We offer customized solutions for beauty brands, corporate business gifts and furniture matching glass products.
    In addition, we supply full-range daily glassware and packaging containers for large-scale events, festival promotions, event planning agencies and e-commerce merchants.

Material Introduction Material

  • Soda-Lime Glass
  • Borosilicate Glass
  • Crystal Glass

Soda-Lime Glass

  • Low cost and high cost performance.
  • Rich styles and diverse designs.
  • Easy molding and mass production available.
  • High transparency for basic daily use.
  • Light weight and comfortable to hold.
  • Wide application for tableware and decoration.
  • Stable property under normal room temperature.
  • Perfect for cold drink and dry food storage.

 

Borosilicate Glass

  • Excellent thermal shock resistance.
  • Food-grade lead-free safe material.
  • Suitable for oven, microwave and disinfection.
  • High temperature resistant for heating use.
  • Ideal for tea set, kitchenware and storage.
  • Corrosion resistant and chemical stable.
  • Ultra clear texture, never turn cloudy.
  • Better drop resistance than ordinary glass.

 

Crystal Glass

  • High light transmittance and diamond luster,Brilliant light refraction effect.
  • Heavy hand feel with premium texture.
  • Clear and melodious tapping sound.
  • Exquisite cutting surface and artistic sense.
  • High-grade for banquet and formal occasion.
  • Perfect luxury gift and collection value.
  • Elegant shape enhances lifestyle taste.
  • Lead-free crystal safe for daily drinking.
